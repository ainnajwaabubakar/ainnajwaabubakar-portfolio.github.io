Order-Go Food Ordering System

Project Description

By using this system, students can order their meals without having to queue or visit a stall just to order food or drinks. This system will provide user-friendly interface where they can find all kinds of cafes in UniSZA and they will get all the menus from each cafe. Students can order food from anywhere and this system will provide estimation time for food to be ready.

Technologies Used

Firstly, as a developer, in order to ensure the users will experience a good system, I make use of 'machine learning algorithm' which including many factors such as order volume, location of the cafe and availability of the cooks. Secondly, this system provided 'in-app payments' to fulfill on-demand app with secure payment integration such as PayPal.
To visit this app interface, kindly click here.

Project Objectives

  • To place the meals order without having to queue or visit a stall.
  • To provide sellers with manageable order lists
  • To properly manage time in food ordering at cafe.
